Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard

The Business Problem

A retail chain operating across 4 US regions was losing profitability despite growing revenue. Built a complete end-to-end data analytics project using Power BI and Tableau to uncover the root cause — over-discounting and underperforming product categories — across 9,994 real sales orders spanning 3 years (2021–2023).

  • Power BI: 4-page interactive dashboard with DAX measures, Date Table & time intelligence
  • Tableau Public: 5-sheet story dashboard — Sales Trend, Category Profit, Geo Heatmap, Discount Impact & Segment Analysis
  • Discount impact analysis proving higher discounts = negative profit (key business insight)
  • Geographic profit analysis across US states and 4 regions
  • KPI cards showing $29.7M revenue, $1.24M profit, 4.2% margin, 10K orders
